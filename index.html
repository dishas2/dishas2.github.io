The Nectarine Frangipane Tart

This delicious tart is a wonderful summery dessert and a good introduction to the world of pastry! The pastry is perfectly buttery and flakey, and relatively easy to make. If you run into issues with the dough, add flour if too wet and a bit of water if breaking apart. The sweet almond filling and the fragrant nectarines make this tart heavenly.
